BITVAS: Bitcoin Visual Analytics System

COMBINED VISUALIZATION

The Combined Visualization shows Blocks in the Timestamp period chosen in the left field above. The reference period is from 12 hours before and 12 hours after this value.

It allows to focus on Timestamp for:

  • Blocks analysis
  • Blocks relationship (transactions information)
  • General information

Click on a Miner in the legend to go to Miner Visualization
Click on a Block to go to Block Visualization

MINER VISUALIZATION

The Miner Visualization lets you focus on a specific Miner showing only blocks validated by him in the Timestamp period.

Some miners are ViaBTC, Bitfury, AntPool, SlushPool, Poolin and Unknown when it's unknown.

BLOCK VISUALIZATION

The Block Visualization lets you focus on a specific Block showing all its relationship with other blocks.

You can click on a Block in the Combined Visualization or search the hash or block ID in the right field above.

TRANSACTION VISUALIZATION

The Transaction Visualization lets you focus on a specific Transaction showing its input and output and all its informations like the value.

You can search the hash of the transaction in the right field above.
